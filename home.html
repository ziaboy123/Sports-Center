Welcome to Brunel University Sports Centre

Brunel University Sports Centre was established to provide students, staff, and the community with top-notch sports and fitness facilities. It offers state-of-the-art gym equipment, an Olympic-sized swimming pool, and a variety of indoor and outdoor sports courts.

Since its inception, the sports centre has been a hub for promoting physical well-being, encouraging active lifestyles, and hosting university-level sports events. Our mission is to create an inclusive environment where individuals of all fitness levels can thrive.
