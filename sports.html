Sports Center

Our state-of-the-art sports facilities rank among the best university facilities in the UK and are used by some of the world's elite athletes. Our facilities are open to students, staff, and members of the community. Activity programmes run throughout the year but with seasonal variations. We welcome anyone with disabilities and aim to provide a welcoming and friendly environment that is supportive to all our users with lift and ramp access. We are committed to providing opportunities for all to take part in sport and active recreation. Please contact us for further inquiries.

Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced game played with a shuttlecock and rackets. It is a popular sport among students. Students can join the game on Mondays and Wednesdays from 5-7 PM at the Sports Centre.

Basketball

Basketball is a team sport that requires skill and strategy. The team practices every Tuesday and Thursday from 6-8 PM. Players are selected through trials in the first week of each semester.
